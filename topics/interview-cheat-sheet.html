05Prompts to surface.

Things to bring up unprompted to signal seniority:

DomainWhat to say
Idempotency"Mutations need idempotency keys; this matters because retries are at-least-once."
Failure modes"What happens when this dependency is slow? Circuit breaker; bounded queue; cached fallback."
Monitoring"Key metrics: p99 latency, error rate, queue depth, cache hit rate. SLO on top."
Cost"At this scale, the cost line is X; we can save by Y at the expense of Z."
Security"Auth at the gateway; rate-limit; secrets in a manager; mTLS internally."
Migration / rollback"Forward-only migrations; feature-flag the new path; old code handles both schemas."
Scope cuts"For v1, I'd drop X and Y; they're complex and not core."
"The cheat sheet isn't a script. It's a reminder of what to surface when the moment matches."
